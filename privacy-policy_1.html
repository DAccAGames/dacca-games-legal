2. Information collection and use

2.1 Information you provide directly

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, physical address or phone number directly from you. The Application is free to play and currently contains no in-app purchases or microtransactions. You may optionally enter a display name to appear on leaderboards. If you contact us by email, we receive the information contained in your message, including your email address.

2.2 Information collected automatically by third-party services

The Application uses third-party services that may collect information which can be used to identify your device, provide core functionality, and serve advertisements:

  • Advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID) — a resettable identifier used to serve and measure advertisements.
  • Device and technical information — device model and name, operating system version, language, app version, and network information such as your IP address.
  • Approximate location — derived from your IP address by advertising and analytics providers. The Application does not request GPS permission and does not track your precise location.
  • Usage and diagnostic data — app opens, session length, in-game events such as levels completed, crash reports and performance data.

2.3 Google Play Games Services (optional)

The Application offers an optional sign-in through Google Play Games Services. Signing in is not required, and the Game is fully playable without it. If you sign in, Google provides us with your Player ID and basic public gamer profile, and the following data is associated with your Google account:

  • your saved game progress (cloud save), so you can continue across devices;
  • achievements you have unlocked;
  • leaderboard scores, if you take part in leaderboards.

This information is processed by Google under the Google Privacy Policy. You can sign out at any time, and you can view, manage or delete your Play Games data at play.google.com/games/profile.

2.4 Data stored on your device

Your level progress, stars, in-game currency, unlocked themes and settings are saved locally on your device. This data stays on your device unless you sign in to Google Play Games and use the cloud save feature. Uninstalling the Application removes the locally stored data.

You can remove your data in the following ways:

  • Request deletion from us: write to dacca.games.official@gmail.com with the subject line "Data deletion request", telling us which data you want removed. We will respond within 30 days.

Because the Application does not create an account with us, we normally hold no data that identifies you directly. If you have signed in with Google Play Games, deleting that data through your Play Games profile is the fastest and most complete method.
